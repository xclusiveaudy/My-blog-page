Hello Everyone

Hey listen

Be yourself. Be different.

Don’t let them tell you who to be.

Be the person they can say is different

Be the cheetah in a place full of lions

Be the splash of color in a world of gray

Love yourself for the color you are

Love yourself for the creativity

Stand on the different side of the wall

The wall of “perfection”

A place that everyone wants you to stand at

A place to never want to stay at

But a place where you never belonged

A place you never wanted to be at

And no you not the problem

Actually you are the problem

The problem that wants to stand out

The problem that is different

The problem that is not perfect

Be the person who you want to be

Not the person people want you to be.

By Autumn Worthy
